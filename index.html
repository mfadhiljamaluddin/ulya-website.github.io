About us


Bookstore since 1985

PERIPLUS was founded in 1985 to provide high-quality imported books and magazines to readers in Indonesia. Through the years, our network has grown and we now have over 56 retail outlets in strategic shopping areas around Indonesia including airports and malls. And our latest outlet is at Petitenget, Bali.

PERIPLUS BOOKSTORES make the latest air-flown imported books and magazines available in an attractive environment. Our staffs are trained to be knowledgeable and helpful.

PERIPLUS E-KIOSK TERMINALS allow in-store staff to assist you in ordering any book or magazine from around the world and have it delivered directly to your home or office.

PERIPLUS.COM ONLINE BOOKSTORE enables you to select from over 21 million international books and magazines with fast, guaranteed delivery and low prices.

Shipping


Introduction

Currently, Periplus.com delivers only to all addresses within Indonesia. We can also accept overseas orders, but we won’t accept for now requests for overseas delivery (outside of Indonesia) except their intended shipping addresses are within Indonesia.


Delivery Charges & Delivery Times

Some items on our website are already available in Indonesia and other need to be imported from overseas. Delivery charges and times will vary depending on the delivery option you selected on the Checkout page. If you order more than one item, you have the following options:


- Send all of my items in one shipment : Choosing this option will be cheaper and save you some money on shipping. Since we are consolidating your items into one shipment, Delivery Times depend on the availability of the all items you ordered, and you might have to wait for all of your order to arrive.


- I want my item faster : Please ship items as they become available – Choosing this option will get your items shipped as soon as they available, especially those items which already in stock locally. Shipping costs will be calculated separately for each delivery, but you will get some of your items earlier and faster compare with another option.


- Free Shipping: Aside from the area within Jabodetabek, we also offer a FREE SHIPPING for those places located within the Greater Java Areas for a minimum total purchase: Rp 100,000, if shipping address located within the JABODETABEK, Rp 300,000, for other places beyond JABODETABEK but within the greater Java areas.


Order Tracking

You can track the status of your order by checking through your Order History page using the tracking reference stated in our confirmation email. If the status of your order is already processed, Tracking Reference will be provided which you can use to track your shipment through the delivery provider website.


Restrictions

Periplus.com delivers to all addresses within Indonesia but we also impose restrictions and could not fulfill delivery through the following addresses: Post Office Boxes address, Incorrect Shipping Address indicated during your checkout process, Address outside Indonesia.


Items Availability

The In Stock status shown on the product detail page tells you that we have a sufficient amount locally ready to serve our online and offline outlets until it reached the threshold quantity and it will become Out of Stock.Placing an item in your shopping cart doesn’t necessarily hold the item for you, it will only reserve for you until you click the Place Your Order button, finish the checkout process and you get the email confirmation that we’ve already received your order. Best selling items can sell out quickly, and it is possible that the availability status in product details would change suddenly while placing your order or before finishing your checkout process. If the item’s status became out of stock after you place your order, we’ll immediately send you a notification email.

Packaging

All items are carefully packaged at our warehouse before shipment. We ensure the package is secure enough to avoid any damage. We wrap your items using our special packaging then wrap it with plastic outside and place it in a corrugated carton.


Delivery Service Delays

We always make sure that all of your items are delivered within the estimated delivery date. However, due to certain conditions like severe weather, flight delays and other natural disaster, delivery of items can sometimes be delayed. If this happens, please give us an allowance of at least 2 to 3 business days from the estimated delivery date of your order to fulfill our delivery.


Undeliverable Packages

Occasionally, Carrier returns to us with UNDELIVERABLE package and we always make sure that we contact the owner and notify for new delivery instructions. If the item returned to us twice as UNDELIVERABLE, it wouldn’t possible for us to re-ship the item to the same address. You will be needed to give us a different or corrected address. If you think that your order was not delivered to your proper address and you have not received any confirmation of return a week after the Estimated Delivery Date, please don’t hesitate to contact us and submit the Contact Us form.
